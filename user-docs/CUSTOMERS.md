## üõçÔ∏è Ordering

### How to Place an Order

1. **Browse Vendors**
   - Open the app and browse available vendors in your area
   - Use search to find specific stores or items
   - Filter by category (food, groceries, pharmacy, etc.)

2. **Select Items**
   - Tap on a vendor to view their catalog
   - Add items to your cart
   - Customize items if options are available
   - View item details, prices, and descriptions

3. **Multi-Vendor Ordering**
   - Continue shopping from other vendors
   - All items go into one cart for a single delivery
   - Save on delivery fees with combined orders

4. **Review Cart**
   - Check all items and quantities
   - Apply promo codes if available
   - View subtotal, delivery fee, and total

5. **Checkout**
   - Select delivery address
   - Choose delivery time (now or schedule for later)
   - Select payment method
   - Add delivery instructions if needed
   - Confirm and place order

### Scheduled Deliveries
- Plan ahead by scheduling deliveries

## üì¶ Pickup & Delivery Requests

Beyond food and shopping, TukShopp lets you send and receive items anywhere!

### What is Pickup & Delivery?

Send documents, packages, gifts, or any items to friends, family, or business contacts without ordering from a vendor.

### How to Request Pickup & Delivery

#### Step 1: Create Request

1. Open TukShopp app
2. Tap on "Send Package" or "Pickup & Delivery"
3. Choose request type:
   - **Send Item** - You have an item to send
   - **Receive Item** - Someone is sending you something

#### Step 2: Pickup Details

**Pickup Location:**
- Enter pickup address
- Add contact name and phone number
- Provide landmark or special instructions
- Choose immediate or scheduled pickup

**Package Information:**
- Item description (required)
- Package size (Small, Medium, Large)
- Weight estimate
- Special handling (Fragile, Keep upright, etc.)
- Photo of item (optional but recommended)

#### Step 3: Delivery Details

**Drop-off Location:**
- Enter delivery address
- Add recipient name and phone number
- Delivery instructions
- Preferred delivery time

**Multiple Drop-offs:**
- Add multiple delivery locations
- Items delivered in sequence
- Separate pricing for each stop

#### Step 4: Review & Pay

- View instant pricing breakdown
- Choose payment method (Card, Wallet, Cash)
- Review pickup and delivery details
- Confirm and request rider

### Pricing

**Based on:**
- Distance between pickup and delivery
- Package size and weight
- Delivery urgency (Standard or Express)
- Multiple drop-off points
- Time of day (peak hour surcharge may apply)

**Estimate:**
- Get instant price quote before confirming
- No hidden charges
- Transparent pricing

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Can I order from multiple vendors at once?
Yes! TukShopp allows you to order from multiple vendors in a single transaction.

### How much is delivery?
Delivery fees vary by distance and zone. You'll see the exact fee before checkout.

### Can I schedule an order for later?
Yes, you can schedule deliveries for specific times that work for you.

### What if an item is unavailable?
Vendors will contact you for substitutions or refunds if items are unavailable.

### How do I cancel an order?
Open your order in the app and tap "Cancel Order" before it's picked up by a rider.

### Can I contact my rider?
Yes, once a rider is assigned, you can call them through the app.
