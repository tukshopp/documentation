### General Questions

#### What is TukShopp?
TukShopp is a delivery platform that connects customers with local vendors through an efficient, zone-based delivery network. Order from multiple vendors and get everything delivered together.

#### Which areas does TukShopp serve?
We currently operate in [list of cities/areas]. Check the app to see if we deliver to your location. We're constantly expanding!

#### How does TukShopp work?
1. Browse vendors in your area
2. Add items to your cart (from one or multiple vendors)
3. Checkout and pay
4. Track your order in real-time
5. Receive your delivery!

### Ordering

#### Can I order from multiple vendors at once?
Yes! That's one of TukShopp's best features. Add items from different vendors to your cart and they'll all be delivered together.

#### What types of items can I order?
- Food from restaurants and cafes
- Groceries
- Pharmacy items (coming soon)
- Retail products
- And more!

#### Is there a minimum order amount?
Minimum order amounts vary by vendor. You'll see any minimum requirements on the vendor's page.

#### Can I schedule a delivery for later?
Yes! You can schedule deliveries for specific times that suit you. Select "Schedule for later" during checkout.

#### How do I cancel an order?
Open your order and tap "Cancel Order" before it's picked up by a rider. Refunds are processed according to our refund policy.

### Delivery

#### How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by location and demand, typically 30-45 minutes. You'll see an estimated delivery time before placing your order.

#### How can I track my order?
Use the live tracking feature in the app to see your rider's location in real-time and get updates on your order status.

#### What if I'm not home when the delivery arrives?
You can add delivery instructions (leave at door, call on arrival, etc.) during checkout. Contact your rider through the app if plans change.

#### Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
Contact support immediately if you need to change your address. We'll do our best to accommodate if the order hasn't been picked up yet.

#### What if my order is wrong or incomplete?
Report the issue immediately through: Orders ‚Üí Select Order ‚Üí Report Issue. We'll work to resolve it quickly.

### Payments

#### What payment methods are accepted?
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Cash on delivery
- TukShopp Wallet
- Digital wallets (coming soon)

#### How does the TukShopp Wallet work?
The TukShopp Wallet is your digital wallet for instant payments. Load money via card or bank transfer, then use it for faster checkout without entering card details each time.

#### How do I add money to my wallet?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Wallet ‚Üí Top Up. Enter the amount (minimum ‚Ç¶500) and choose your payment method. Funds are added instantly.

#### Can I withdraw money from my wallet?
Yes! You can withdraw wallet balance back to your bank account. Minimum withdrawal is ‚Ç¶1,000, and it takes 1-3 business days to process.

#### Is it safe to save my card information?
Yes! All payment information is encrypted and securely stored. We're PCI-DSS compliant.

#### When am I charged?
You're charged when you place your order. For cash on delivery, payment is collected at delivery.

#### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are issued for cancelled orders (before pickup) or valid issues with your order. Wallet refunds are instant; card refunds process within 5-7 business days.

#### Where do refunds go?
Refunds are automatically credited to your TukShopp Wallet for instant access. You can use the balance for future orders or withdraw to your bank account.

#### Can I tip my rider?
Yes! You can add a tip during checkout or after delivery. Riders keep 100% of tips.

### Pickup & Delivery

#### How do I send a package using TukShopp?
Tap "Send Package" or "Pickup & Delivery" in the app, enter pickup and delivery details, specify package information, review pricing, and confirm. A rider will be assigned to pickup and deliver your item.

#### What can I send using Pickup & Delivery?
You can send documents, gifts, personal items, electronics (properly packaged), clothing, books, and more. Illegal items, hazardous materials, live animals, and weapons are prohibited.

#### How much does Pickup & Delivery cost?
Pricing is based on distance, package size (Small, Medium, Large), urgency (standard or express), and multiple drop-offs. You'll see the exact price before confirming your request.

#### Can I send packages to multiple locations?
Yes! You can add multiple drop-off points, and items will be delivered in sequence with separate pricing for each stop.

#### How do I track my package?
Once a rider is assigned, you can track the package in real-time on the map, see status updates, and contact the rider directly through the app.

#### What are the package size limits?
- Small: Up to 2kg (documents, phones, small items)
- Medium: 2-10kg (laptops, clothes, electronics)
- Large: 10-25kg (boxes, appliances, bulky items)

#### Can I schedule a pickup for later?
Yes! You can schedule pickups for a specific time that works for you when creating your request.

#### What's the difference between Standard and Express delivery?
Standard delivery is cost-effective with normal timeframes. Express delivery offers priority handling and faster delivery for urgent items at a higher fee.

#### How does proof of delivery work?
The rider takes a photo and collects a signature (when applicable) upon delivery. You receive delivery confirmation with timestamp and location verification.

### Account

#### Do I need an account to order?
Yes, you need to create an account to place orders. It's quick and easy!

#### How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions sent to your email/phone.

#### Can I have multiple delivery addresses?
Yes! Save all your frequent locations (home, work, etc.) for quick selection.

#### How do I delete my account?
Contact support to request account deletion. Note that this action is permanent.

## üö¥ For Riders

### Becoming a Rider

#### How do I sign up as a rider?
Visit tukshopp.ng and click "Join as a rider" or download the Rider app and apply.

#### What are the requirements?
- Age 18 or above
- Valid driver's license/permit
- Smartphone with GPS
- Reliable vehicle (motorcycle, bicycle, or car)
- Clean background check

#### How long does approval take?
Once you submit all documents, approval typically takes 1-3 business days.

#### Do I need my own vehicle?
Yes, riders must have their own reliable vehicle and necessary insurance.

### Working as a Rider

#### How do I start working?
Open the Rider app and tap "Go Online" to start receiving delivery requests.

#### Can I choose when to work?
Yes! TukShopp offers complete flexibility. Work whenever you want by going online.

#### How are deliveries assigned to me?
Orders are matched based on proximity, zone assignment, availability, and performance metrics.

#### Can I reject deliveries?
Yes, but high rejection rates may affect how many orders you receive. Only reject when necessary.

#### What are zones?
Zones are geographic areas that help optimize deliveries. You select which zones you want to work in.

### Earnings

#### How much can I earn?
Earnings vary based on deliveries completed, distance, tips, bonuses, and hours worked.

#### When do I get paid?
Weekly payouts every Monday via direct deposit. Instant cashout is available for a small fee.

#### Do I keep all my tips?
Yes! You keep 100% of tips from customers.

#### Are there bonuses?
Yes! Earn bonuses for peak hours, high performance, weather conditions, and completing incentive programs.
