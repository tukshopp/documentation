## â“ Common Questions

### How are zone boundaries determined?
Based on geographic data, vendor density, customer demand, traffic patterns, and delivery efficiency analysis.

### Can zones change?
Yes, zones are periodically reviewed and adjusted based on performance data and growth.

### What if I'm on a zone boundary?
You'll have access to vendors from multiple zones, potentially more options!

### Do riders have to stay in one zone?
No, riders can work multiple zones and accept cross-zone deliveries.

### Why might a vendor not serve my zone?
Vendors choose zones based on their capacity and delivery radius preferences.

### Are there additional fees for cross-zone delivery?
Delivery fees account for distance and may be higher for cross-zone deliveries.
