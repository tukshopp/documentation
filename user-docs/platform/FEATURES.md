- Search by vendor name, item, or category
- Filter by cuisine, rating, distance, delivery time

- High-resolution product images

#### Scheduling
- Order now or schedule for later
- Available time slots displayed
- Edit scheduled orders before processing
- Recurring order scheduling (coming soon)

#### Pickup & Delivery Requests
- **Item Pickup**: Request pickup of items from any location
- **Item Delivery**: Send items to friends, family, or business contacts
- **Flexible pickup locations**: Home, office, or any address
- **Multiple drop-off points**: Deliver to one or multiple locations
- **Package details**: Specify size, weight, and contents
- **Real-time tracking**: Track pickup and delivery in real-time
- **Instant pricing**: Get delivery cost before confirming
- **Proof of delivery**: Photo confirmation and signatures
- **Scheduled pickups**: Plan pickups for later times
- **Express delivery**: Priority delivery options available
